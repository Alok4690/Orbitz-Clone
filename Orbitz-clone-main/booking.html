About this property

The Leela palace New Delhi

4 restaurants, a full-service spa, and an outdoor pool are available at this hotel. Free WiFi in public
areas and free valet parking are also provided. Additionally, a health club, a bar/lounge, and a sauna
are onsite. All 254 shared rooms provide free WiFi, free wired Internet, and memory foam beds. Guests
can enjoy deep soaking tubs, and other standard amenities include iPod docks, sitting areas, and 24-
hour room service.
